3D unit block visual:
- Base dimensions: 1.5 × 0.5 × 1.0 (width × height × depth)
- Width scaled by ships/300 (one unit = 300 ships), clamped 0.3-3.0
- Empire = blue (#4466ff) MeshStandardMaterial, Alliance = red (#ff4444)
- Flagship: BoxGeometry slightly taller (height 0.8), yellow EmissiveColor
- Grid: PlaneGeometry 100×100, GridHelper blue lines (#1a3060)
- BEAM effect: Line3D between two unit positions, yellow (#ffff00), duration 0.3s
- Missile effect: small Sphere moving along lerp path over 0.5s
- Explosion: PointLight flash + scale pulse on hit unit
