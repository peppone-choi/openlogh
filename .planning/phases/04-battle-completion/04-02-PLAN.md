Formula chain for computeWarPower (BattleEngine.kt lines 687-955):
1. Base: ARM_PER_PHASE (500) + myAttack - opDefence
2. Floor: if warPower < 100, apply smoothing
3. Atmos/Train: warPower *= atmos / max(1, train)
4. Dex log: warPower *= getDexLog(attackerDex, defenderDex)
5. CrewType coefs: attack coefficient on own, defence coefficient as oppose
6. Exp level scaling
7. Variation: warPower * (0.9 + rng.nextDouble() * 0.2)
8. Oppose multiplier
9. Critical/Dodge/Magic rolls
10. Damage triggers: attackMultiplier, defenceMultiplier
11. Overkill normalization
